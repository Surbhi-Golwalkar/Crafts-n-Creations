Scrap-Booking/Jounaling Ideas


Scrapbooking is a fun way to practice arts and crafts and keep memories in tact. Learn how to scrapbook like a pro with our best tips and ideas.




Scrapbooking is a popular hobby that can bring out your most creative side, and it's an easy way to capture and share your favorite memories.ou'll find all the materials you need in your favorite craft store, including a scrapbook album to start your venture. Scrapbook albums are typically 12 inches by 12 inches, but they come in all different sizes, colors, and styles. Some may even come with a few pages to begin your project. Once you start, you'll likely want to add refill pages to expand your album.Here're some ideas for scrapbook page layout above to get you started.






Since there are SO many fun ways to add interest and variety to your layouts, we thought we would share some of our favorite techniques. If you feel like you’ve run out of ways to creatively scrapbook your memories – don’t give up just yet! Check out the scrapbooking ideas that we’ve rounded up below, get inspired and tackle a new technique with your next layout! Enjoy!

A best friend scrapbook is the ultimate way to show your BFF they’re number one no matter what. Place all your precious memories into one scrapbook album to reminisce in years to come.
